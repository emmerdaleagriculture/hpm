Our story

Founded by people
who actually love this work.

Hampshire Paddock Management started because we kept hearing the same story from horse owners: big contractors turning up with the wrong kit, leaving ruts, rushing the job, or not turning up at all.

We built something different. Compact John Deere tractors on grass tyres, £100,000 of carefully chosen implements, and operators who've been around machinery since school. When we agree a Tuesday, we're there on Tuesday. When we leave, your paddock looks like we cared about it — because we did.

— Tom & Martyn
