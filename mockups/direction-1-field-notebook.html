— About us

Founded by people
who use this kit every day.

We set out to be the contractor we'd want to hire ourselves.

Hampshire Paddock Management was founded by Tom Oswald and Martyn Broad. Tom's been around machinery since the age of eleven — built a hydraulic crane for his GCSE, a robotic arm for his A-levels, and had his first patent by twenty-one. Martyn's been farming for thirty years.

Between us, we've seen how most contractors work. Big tractors on agricultural tyres leaving ruts. Rushed jobs. "Sorry, can't make Tuesday." We built this business to do the opposite: compact machinery, grass tyres, careful work, and if we say we'll be there Tuesday, we'll be there Tuesday.
