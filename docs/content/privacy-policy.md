## What this policy covers

This policy explains what personal information we collect when you use our website (hampshirepaddockmanagement.com), why we collect it, how we use it, and what rights you have. It applies only to this website — when you follow links to other sites, their own privacy policies apply.

## The information we collect

We keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the business. Specifically:

**When you submit our contact or quote form, we collect:**
- Your name
- Your email address
- Your phone number (if you provide it)
- The address or location of the land you're asking about
- Any details you include in your message

**When you visit the website, our servers automatically record:**
- Your IP address and approximate location (country/region)
- The pages you visit and the date/time of your visit
- Your browser type and device type

This automatic information is used solely for security, fraud prevention, and to keep the site running properly. We do not use it to build profiles of individual visitors.

**We do not use third-party analytics, tracking pixels, advertising cookies, or social-media trackers.** The only cookies on this website are essential cookies that keep the site functioning correctly. Because we use essential cookies only, no cookie consent banner is required.

## Why we use your information

We only use your personal information for the following purposes:

1. **To respond to your enquiry** — providing a quote, arranging a site visit, or answering your question. This is the main reason anyone submits information to us.
2. **To carry out the work** — if you become a customer, we use your contact and address information to schedule, deliver, and invoice for the work.
3. **To keep records required by law** — UK tax law requires us to retain invoice and transaction records for at least six years.
4. **To keep the website secure** — we monitor server logs for suspicious activity.

We do not send marketing emails, we do not sell data, and we do not share enquiries with any third party except the small number of service providers listed below.

## Legal basis

Under UK GDPR we rely on the following lawful bases for processing your data:

- **Performance of a contract** — for processing your information when we provide services you've asked us to provide.
- **Legitimate interests** — for responding to enquiries and keeping the website secure. We've considered your rights and freedoms and are satisfied that our interests don't override them.
- **Legal obligation** — for retaining financial records.

## How long we keep your information

- **Enquiries that don't become customers:** deleted or anonymised within 12 months of your last contact.

- **Website server logs:** retained for 30 days.

## Your rights under UK GDPR

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Correct** data that's wrong or incomplete
- **Delete** your data (where no legal obligation requires us to keep it)
- **Object** to our processing, or ask us to restrict it
- **Receive a copy** of your data in a common format
- **Withdraw consent** where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, email [tom@hampshirepaddockmanagement.com](mailto:tom@hampshirepaddockmanagement.com). We'll respond within 30 days.

If you're unhappy with how we've handled your data, you can complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office at **[ico.org.uk](https://ico.org.uk)** or on **0303 123 1113**. We'd ask you to contact us first so we can put things right.
